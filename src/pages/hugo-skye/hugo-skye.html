Hugo Skye Lounge

Hugo’s Skye Lounge (or Sierra del Oro?) is mainly a long stretch of dining options with a good overlooking view of the city. They have a restaurant, a snack shack, and a coffee shop. They also have a mini zoo that contains several birds, reptiles and a Tiger. You have to pay a Php 50 entrance fee for the whole area though.

They also offer a paragliding activity, but the availability will depend on the weather condition and if the winds are favorable and strong enough for such activity.

Aside from the Skye Lounge, Larry’s Hill is also located just a few blocks away from it. Now this is a different area which also requires another entrance fee (Php 50), and it is mostly an activity center for kids and every young adventurous soul. They have a ball pit, a wall climbing and rapeling area, and an obstacle course of hanging ropes.
